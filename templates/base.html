COLORIZE

An AI approach to color black and white images.

Abstract

Colorize is a computer vision algorithm which is implemented as a feed-forward pass in a CNN at test time, that has been trained on a million images from the Imagenet dataset. The algorithm is able to fill vibrant and realistic colors to the black and white images to recreate a plausible colorized image.

Based on the 2016 research paper of Zhang et al.
